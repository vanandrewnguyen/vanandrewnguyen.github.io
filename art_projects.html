Art Projects

I occasionally dabble in art and design. Here are some major pieces I've worked on outside of asset creation for my game dev.

Pixel Art

Sydney Trains Postcard


This was a gift I made, depicting a very typical view of Sydney's train doors. I played with subtle dither patterns to blend the background clouds and gradient.

Hoodie Design


I played with framing and composition to extend the artwork downwards to lead the viewer's eyes. This was for a university hoodie design competition, I wanted an aesthetically pleasing but characteristically intense piece. The hands in the middle show the host's logo.

Character Design 1


Character study involving volumes with more depth. I limited the volume palette to two colours, and to create a more impactful character I decided to give it a series of faces.

Character Design 2


Character study involving posing. I tried breaking up the volumes to support the aesthetic flow of the pose, rather than leave it in one chunky piece.

Spooktober 2018 Art Challenge


Basic experimentation with two toned dithering, to create texture and small details for a tiny canvas.

Spooktober 2019 Art Challenge


Extended use of dither and geometric shape to imply the presence of material.
